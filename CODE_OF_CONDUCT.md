# Code of Conduct

This project is focused exclusively on the technical advancement of the web. All interactions must remain professional and focused on code, architecture, and project goals.

## Rules of Engagement
1.  **Technical Merit:** Decisions are made based on technical evidence and alignment with the "Anti-Slop" philosophy.
2.  **Professionalism:** Debates must be civil and constructive. Personal attacks or unrelated digressions are out of scope.
3.  **Project Integrity:** Maintainers reserve the right to reject contributions that introduce complexity, bloat, or violate the core principles defined in `SPEC.md`.

Failure to adhere to these rules may result in restricted access to the project's communication channels.
